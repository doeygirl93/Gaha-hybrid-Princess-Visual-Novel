# Gacha Hybrid Princess VN 
ITCH.IO LINK: https://doey-girl.itch.io/gachahybridacademy
So This is a Visual novel game made with renpy
## Description
The purpose of making this novel was to revive gacha life and make a fun interactive game for people to play. THis game also aims to teach people that if you don't stand up for yourself you will fail.
## StoryLine 
Your the MC of this excited new Gacha Game. FOr years you've been told your a loser with no potentail. Yet this year of school is different. Because of the war you're school has basically became the next draft. Everybody that was kept in there has been evaluted for having high potential. AND YOU GOT IN! Now its your choice on what your going to do in this academy. But beware as there are bullies and evil people lurking in your very own halllways. You either drop out of the acdemy or make it to the yoai or yuri ending.
## Development stack
This is made through renpy and the art is from Gacha Life. Took 5 hours and 5 minutes
